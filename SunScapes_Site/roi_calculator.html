Metric Explanations:

Monthly Cash Flow:
The net amount of cash you earn or lose each month. It's calculated as (Monthly Rental Income - Monthly Expenses - Monthly Mortgage Payment).
Cash-on-Cash ROI:
Measures the annual return on the actual cash invested. It's calculated as (Annual Cash Flow / Total Cash Invested).
Capitalization Rate (Cap Rate):
Estimates the rate of return on a property if purchased with cash. It's calculated as (Annual Net Operating Income / Purchase Price).
